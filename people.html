LDPL People

This is a list of the people behind the LDPL project.

Main Contributors

Lartu (Martín del Río)

Project admin and lead developer. Creator of the LDPL programming language.

  • Website: https://www.lartu.net/

  • Contact: martin@ldpl-lang.org

Damián Garro

Lead contributor and developer. Heavily refactored the LDPL codebase and contributed a ton to the project. LDPL wouldn't be what it is today without Damián's support and constant enthusiasm.

  • Website: https://github.com/dgarrodc/

dvkt

Contributor. dvkt was among the first contributors to the project and added many core features and statements to the language. Missing in action, then found again. As with Damián, LDPL certainly wouldn't be what it is today without dkvt's vision and help.

  • Website: https://github.com/dvkt/

Special Mentions

FireofGods

Contributor. Among other important contributions, added the include statement to the language so other files could be imported.

  • Website: https://github.com/FireofGods/

Iglosiggio (Ignacio Losiggio)

Contributor. Developed the LDPL Web Extensions.

  • Website: https://github.com/iglosiggio

  • Contact: iglosiggio@gmail.com
